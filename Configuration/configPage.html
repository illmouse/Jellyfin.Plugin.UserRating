Database Health

Check the ratings database for consistency. Ratings with stale item IDs (items no longer in the library) can be healed by matching provider IDs (IMDB, TMDB, TVDB) or cleared.

Recoverable Items

These ratings can be healed by matching their provider IDs (IMDB, TMDB, TVDB) to items in your library.

Healed Items

These ratings were successfully re-linked to items in your library.

Stale Items
